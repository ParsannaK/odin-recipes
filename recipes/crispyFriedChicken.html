Crispy Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

Crispy Fried Chicken is a classic dish that is loved by many.
It is a simple and delicious recipe that is perfect for any occasion.
This recipe is easy to make and is sure to be a hit with your family and friends.

Ingredients

Here's what you'll need to make the best, crispiest fried chicken of your life:
  • 1 (4 pound) Chicken: cut a four-point whole chicken into pieces
    or purchase four pounds of breasts, drumsticks, and thighs.

  • 1 cup Buttermilk: Acidic butterminlk tenderizes the chicken without
    making it too tough. Also, it helps the flour mixture stick to the chicken.

  • 2 cups all-purpose Flour: All-purpose flour gives the buttermilk and seasoning something
    to stick to, while ensuring a wonderfully crispy crust.

  • Seasoning: A blend of salt, pepper, 1 tsp paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder
    gives the chicken a classic flavor.

  • 2 quarts vegetable oil: Use a neutral oil with a high smoke point, like vegetable oil or canola oil,
    for frying.

Directions

You'll find the step-by-step recipe below
  1. Take your cut up chicken pieces and skin them if you prefer.

  2. Put the flour in a large plastic bag.
    Season the flour with paprika, salt, and pepper to taste.



  3. Dip chicken pieces in buttermilk and put them in the bag with the flour mixture.
    Seal the bag and shake to coat evenly.



  4. Place the coated chicken on a cookie sheet or tray, and cover with a clean dish
    toel or waxed paper. LET SIT UNTIL THE FLOUR IS OF A PASTE-LIKE CONSISTENCY.



  5. Fill a large skillet (cast iron is best) about 1/3 to 1/2 full with vegetable oil.
    Heat until VERY hot.

  6. Put in as many chicken as the skillet can hold. Brown the chicken in HOT oil on both sides.



  7. When browned, reduce heat and cover skillet; let cook for 30 minutes
    Remove cover, raise heat again, and continue to fry until crispy.

  8. Drain the fried chicken on paper towels. Depending on how much chicken you
    have, you make have to fry in few sihfts. Keep the finished chicken in a
    slightly warm oven while preparing the rest.
